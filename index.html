Why we made it?

It is of course good that something like WebAudioAPI was created. However frankly speaking - it is quite primitive...

Which means that for real professional use cases - we should wait for WeAudioAPI version 2.0

Because we needed for ourselves - we created Audiomatic and now we want to open it as well for other developers.
